## About these instructions
The operating instructions contain important information about the assembly and disassembly of the split sprocket wheel 
* Read these operating instructions very carefully and pay attention to the infor-mation contained within, before any maintenance work of the gearbox.
* Ensure that the operating instructions are read and understood by the personnel.
* Contact FLSmidth MAAG Gear AG if you have any questions. We shall be pleased to offer additional help.
&nbsp;

### Purpose of the operating instructions  
The operating instructions will help you to do the maintenance work in a safe and economical way. If you pay attention to the information in these operating
instructions you will:  
* Reduce production downtimes and repair costs  
* Avoid dangerous situations
&nbsp;

### Target groups
&nbsp;

The operating instructions are intended for:  
* Gearbox users  
* Installation and commissioning personnel  
* Operating personnel  
* Maintenance personnel
&nbsp;

### Structure of the gearbox documentation

The operating instructions are part of the gearbox documentation.  
The gearbox documentation contains all the required documents for the proper in-stallation, operation and maintenance of the gearbox.
The gearbox documentation includes at least the following documents:  
* These operating instructions for the safety use split sprocket wheel  
* Declaration of incorporation (if issued)  
* Gearbox data sheet (order-specific)  
* Operating instructions for the coupling  
* Drawings and diagrams  
* Spare parts lists for the main components  
* Operating instructions for other, more complex components
&nbsp;

If optional components are part of our supply, the documentation will be included in the gearbox documentation.
Safe operation of the gearbox and the accompanying components requires that you possess all documentation relating to the gearbox.
The gearbox documentation, assembled individually for each order, is supplied to-gether with the gearbox.
Upon request, generally applicable gearbox documentation can be made available before the gearbox is delivered.
&nbsp;

### Where to keep the instructions
&nbsp;

The instructions are considered to be part of the gearbox until the gearbox is dis-posed of.
Please keep the instructions in a safe place where they can be accessed at all times.
If you sell or pass on the gearbox to another party, be sure to pass on these instructions along with the gearbox.

## Warnings and important information
&nbsp;

Warnings and important information have been highlighted for quick comprehension and for safe operation of the gearbox.
Except for the general safety precautions, the warnings in these instructions always consist of a:
* Danger symbol
* Signal word
* Description of the danger and how it can be avoided

### Meaning of the signal words
A signal word always follows the danger symbol.  
The following signal words are used in these instructions:  
**DANGER** 	Warns that an immediate danger exists. Failure to take action will lead to death or serious injuries.  
**WARNING**	Warns that a potential danger exists. Failure to take action can lead to death or serious injuries.  
**CAUTION**	Warns of a dangerous situation. Failure to take action can lead to minor injuries.  
**NOTICE**	Warns of a damaging situation. Failure to take action can lead to damage to the product or the surrounding area.
