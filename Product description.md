## Function of the auxiliary drive
The auxiliary drive is designated for mill positioning in order to make necessary work associated with its maintenance and inspection.  
The auxiliary drive transmits rotation torque to gearbox input shaft by split sprocket wheel.

=> For more information please view the operating instructions of the auxiliary drive.

## Function of the split sprocket wheel
Split sprocket wheel gives the possibility to operate drive transmission without disassembly of the coupling while maintenance operation only.
